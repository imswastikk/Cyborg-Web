The Official Robotics and Automation Club of NITR


  • Automation

The technique of making an apparatus, a process, or a system operate automatically.

  • Electronics

The branch of physics and technology concerned with the design of circuits using transistors and microchips, and with the behaviour and movement of electrons in a semiconductor, conductor, vacuum, or gas.

  • Mechanical

The branch of applied mathematics dealing with motion and forces producing motion.

  • Robotics

Robotics is the intersection of science, engineering and technology that produces machines, called robots, that substitute for (or replicate) human actions.

  • Web

Web development is the work involved in developing a website for the Internet or an intranet.
